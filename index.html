Our History

At Unravel, we believe that everyone should be able to enjoy a warm, delicious bowl of ramen made just the way they like it and without any restrictions. That’s exactly why we let you customize your own ramen! You will be able to pick from different types of noodles, rich and flavorful broths, and a variety of fresh, tasty toppings, and most importantly, it’s all completely vegetarian. Our goal is to make sure that every bite of the ramen is comforting and satisfying and COMPLETELY meat free.

Our Approach

Our
Approach

Unravel cares about where our ingredients come from and about customer transparency. That’s why we only use fresh, locally sourced produce. Our broths are cooked slowly for rich flavor without animal products. Our noodles are made with high-quality grains, and every topping is fresh, locally sourced, and prepared to bring out its natural taste. (Swipe plates to see images)

Our main goal

At Unravel, we serve great ramen while caring for you and the planet with biodegradable packaging, sustainable cooking, and plant-based ingredients.

Energy-Efficient Cooking

We use sustainable cooking methods to lower energy and water waste.

Supporting Local Farmers

Buying locally means fewer emissions from food transportation.
